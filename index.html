Rabbit

You will be given a target number at the start of the game.

There are four rabbits. By clicking on a rabbit you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the target. You lose the game if your total score goes above target.

The value of each rabbit is hidden from you until you click on it.

The value of each rabbit changes when the game starts.
